Symptoms

Common symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Seek medical attention if you experience severe symptoms.

Prevention

Wash your hands regularly, wear a mask, and practice social distancing to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

Contact

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact your local health authorities or call our helpline.
